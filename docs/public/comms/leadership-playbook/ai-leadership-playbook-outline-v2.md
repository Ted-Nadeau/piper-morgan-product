# The AI Leadership Playbook
## Strawman Chapter Outline v2

**Working subtitle**: What Seven Months of Building with AI Taught Me About Leading It

**Voice**: YouTube craftsman showing the warts-and-all experience—demystifying rather than pontificating, inviting rather than lecturing

**Target audience**: Leaders in general; product and design leaders in tech orgs specifically

**Format**: Series of articles → eventual book

**Core thesis**: AI amplifies whatever methodology you have. The moat is discipline, not tools.
