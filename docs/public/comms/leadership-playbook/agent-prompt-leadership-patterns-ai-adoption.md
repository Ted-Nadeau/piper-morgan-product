## Tone Guidance

This is for leaders, not practitioners. Translate everything up one level of abstraction:

| Implementation Level | Leadership Level |
|---------------------|------------------|
| "Use completion matrices" | "Define 'done' before you start" |
| "Audit against templates" | "Build institutional skepticism into handoffs" |
| "The ConversationRepository was stubbed" | "Infrastructure can exist for months without working" |

The audience has heard "AI will change everything." They need to hear "Here's specifically what changes and what stays the same."
