## 📈 Document Purposes

### Technical Report (project-report.md)
**Audience**: Technical stakeholders, future developers, architectural reviewers
**Purpose**: Comprehensive assessment of technical progress, architectural decisions, and implementation insights
**Key Sections**:
- Current status with completed capabilities vs. gaps
- Technical achievements and architectural sophistication
- Implementation insights and lessons learned
- Performance analysis and scaling considerations
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Path to "1.0" definition with timeline estimates

### Executive Summary (executive-summary.md)  
**Audience**: Business leaders, investment decision makers, non-technical stakeholders
**Purpose**: Business impact assessment and strategic recommendations
**Key Sections**:
- Current operational capabilities
- Business value proposition and ROI analysis
- Investment requirements and scaling considerations
- Risk assessment from business perspective
- Competitive positioning and market opportunity
- Success metrics and timeline for value delivery

### Team Demo Presentation (presentations/team-demo.html)
**Audience**: Technical team members, PM stakeholders, development partners
**Purpose**: Interactive demonstration of working capabilities with technical context
**Demo Flow**:
1. **Slides (8 total)**: Architecture status, achievements, current gaps
2. **Live Web Interface**: Natural language processing, multi-project context, error handling
3. **Q&A**: Technical architecture, AI integration, business value
