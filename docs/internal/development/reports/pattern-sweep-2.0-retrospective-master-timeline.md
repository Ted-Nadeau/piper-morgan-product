## Lessons Learned

### On Pattern Discovery
1. **Crises are gifts** - Every major failure becomes reusable knowledge
2. **Proto-patterns matter** - Track informal practices, they often formalize
3. **Invisibility signals maturity** - When patterns aren't discussed, they're working

### On Completion
1. **75% is the enemy** - Universal anti-pattern requiring cultural countermeasures
2. **Evidence is non-negotiable** - Claims without proof are worthless
3. **Permission to pause saves rework** - Time Lord Alert reduces completion bias

### On Methodology
1. **Inchworm beats sprinting** - Sequential completion prevents abandonment
2. **Multi-agent coordination multiplies** - Different agents catch different issues
3. **Verification before closure** - Serena MCP + Beads = objective truth
