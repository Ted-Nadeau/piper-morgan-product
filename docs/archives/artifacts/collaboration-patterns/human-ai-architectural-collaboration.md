## Collaboration Anti-Patterns to Avoid

### Anti-Pattern 1: Passive Human Authority
**Problem**: Human provides vague direction, accepts AI implementation without verification
**Example**: "Just make it work" → AI implements suboptimal solution → Human accepts without validation
**Solution**: Clear strategic vision, explicit acceptance criteria, active verification

### Anti-Pattern 2: AI Authority Overreach
**Problem**: AI makes strategic architectural decisions without human domain expertise
**Example**: AI chooses technical pattern based on implementation convenience vs strategic fit
**Solution**: AI focuses on systematic execution, human maintains strategic authority

### Anti-Pattern 3: Sequential Instead of Collaborative
**Problem**: Human defines requirements → AI implements → Human accepts (no real-time collaboration)
**Example**: August 5 could have failed without 3:44 PM course correction intervention
**Solution**: Continuous collaboration with real-time verification and course correction

### Anti-Pattern 4: Velocity Over Quality
**Problem**: Speed of implementation compromises strategic requirements or technical quality
**Example**: 6-minute refactoring could break existing functionality or miss requirements
**Solution**: Systematic execution patterns that preserve quality under velocity
