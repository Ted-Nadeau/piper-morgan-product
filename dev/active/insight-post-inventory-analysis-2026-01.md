## Publishing Strategy

### Principles

1. **Pair intentionally** — related pieces deepen a theme, contrasting pieces create variety
2. **Space overlapping themes** — 4-6 weeks between similar arguments
3. **Lead with unique** — distinctive pieces first, variations later
4. **Consider external publication** — some pieces have broader reach potential
