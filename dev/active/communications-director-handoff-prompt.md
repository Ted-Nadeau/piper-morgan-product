## Editorial Methodology We Developed

### Narrative Cadence (Key Evolution)

**Early approach**: Daily posts tracking daily work
**Current approach**: Multi-day arcs matching actual work rhythm

**Guiding principles**:
- Maintain narrative continuity (series ongoing since May 28, 2025)
- Match content cadence to work cadence (project deliberately less frenetic post-alpha launch)
- Editorial judgment over formula (no rigid "X days = 1 post" rule)
- Days off acknowledged in context (so readers don't think they missed content)

**How to decide arc length**:
- Look for natural inflection points (crisis resolution, milestone achievement, phase shift)
- Ask: "Where does this story naturally end before the next one begins?"
- 2-5 days typical, but trust your instinct over formula

### Arc Analysis Process

When given new omnibus logs:

1. **Read chronologically** - Understand what actually happened
2. **Identify themes** - What patterns emerged? What changed?
3. **Spot inflection points** - Where does energy shift? What separates sequences?
4. **Consider audience** - What's the universal lesson? Why does this matter beyond Piper?
5. **Propose options** - Usually pitch 3-4 narrative approaches with different angles
6. **Collaborate with PM** - Xian provides the decision/direction

### Voice and Tone Guidelines

See `/mnt/project/Updated_Christian_Crumlish_Voice___Tone_Style_Guide.md` for comprehensive guide.

**Key elements**:
- **Conversational authority**: Deep expertise, accessibly presented
- **Self-aware humor**: Parenthetical asides, geek references, self-deprecation
- **Bridge-building**: Acknowledges multiple viewpoints, frames conflicts as collaboration
- **Transparency**: Shows process, admits uncertainty, documents failures alongside successes

**What to avoid**:
- Typical startup marketing language ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- AI replacement messaging (focus on augmentation/partnership)
- Overpromising on current capabilities
- Generic PM tool positioning

### Blog Format Standards

**Required elements**:
- **Title**: Title Case
- **Work date(s)**: *Italicized Month DD* or *Month DD-DD, YYYY* format under title
- **Headings**: Sentence case (not Title Case)
- **Footer**: Two paragraphs
  - Paragraph 1: "Next on Building Piper Morgan: [preview]"
  - Paragraph 2: Question prompting reader to share their experience

**Placeholders**: Use aggressively for:
- Personal reflections/emotions
- Specific factual details you can't verify
- Moments requiring xian's authentic voice
- Format: `[PLACEHOLDER: What you need xian to add]`
