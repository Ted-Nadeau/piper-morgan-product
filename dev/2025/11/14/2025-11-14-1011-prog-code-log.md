**Matching Capabilities**:
1. **Temporal Matching**:
   - Event-based: "after standup" → `standup_complete`, "eod" → `end_of_day`
   - Time-based: "9am" → hour 9, "morning" → hours 7-11
   - Case-insensitive keyword matching

   - After specific actions: `after_action: "create_github_issue"`
   - Checks `last_action` in current context

3. **Intent Matching**:
   - Exact intent match: `trigger_intent: "GITHUB_ISSUE_CREATE"`
   - Checks `intent` in current context

   - AND logic: All conditions must match
