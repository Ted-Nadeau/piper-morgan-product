1. ServiceContainerPhase: Initialize DDD container, store in app.state

6. BackgroundCleanupPhase: Start token blacklist cleanup job

### Shutdown Phase Sequence (Reverse Order)
1. BackgroundCleanupPhase.shutdown(): Stop cleanup job
2. PluginInitializationPhase.shutdown(): Shutdown all plugins
3. ServiceContainerPhase.shutdown(): Shutdown service container
