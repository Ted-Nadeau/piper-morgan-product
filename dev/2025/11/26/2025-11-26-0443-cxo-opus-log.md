### The CXO Question

**How do we make the sophisticated architecture *felt* rather than just *functional*?**

The architecture exists. The theoretical grounding is solid. What's missing is the experience layer that communicates:

- Piper is already there (not summoned from void)
- Piper notices things (proactive, not just reactive)
- Piper has spatial understanding (knows where it is)
- Piper has temporal awareness (knows what time it is)
- Piper has good judgment (knows what matters)
