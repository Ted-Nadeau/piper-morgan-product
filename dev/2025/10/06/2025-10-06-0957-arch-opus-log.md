## 4:50 PM - Investigation Complete

### Root Cause Found

- Canonical handlers ARE properly integrated (line 123-131)
- When classifier gets it right → works perfectly
- When classifier mis-classifies (e.g., TEMPORAL as QUERY) → timeout errors

### The Real Architecture
1. **Fast path** (canonical): IDENTITY, TEMPORAL, STATUS, PRIORITY, GUIDANCE
2. **Workflow path**: EXECUTION, ANALYSIS, SYNTHESIS, STRATEGY, LEARNING
3. **Problem**: No QUERY workflow, so mis-classified canonicals fail
