## 🚧 The real journey

### From proof of concept to alpha

Let's be honest about how we got here:

**May 2025**: Proof of concept discussions - could AI really help with PM work?

**June 2025**: Threw away first prototype, restarted with proper DDD architecture

**July 2025**: Built frantically, developed manual processes, started blogging. Excellence Flywheel methodology emerged organically from the work.

**August 2025**: Built a lot but didn't finish, track, or document well. Methodology kept improving while code accumulated chaos.

**September 2025**: Hit a wall. Called an ALL STOP. Evaluated everything. Started the Great Refactor.

**October 2025**: Finished GREAT, started march to alpha, discovered CRAFT gaps, fixed them, and suddenly... velocity.
