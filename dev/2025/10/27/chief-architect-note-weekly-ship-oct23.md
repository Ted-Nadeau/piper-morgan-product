## Methodology Insights

### The 88% Faster Pattern
Across all sprints, actual delivery was 88-92% faster than estimated. This stems from:
1. **Infrastructure leverage** - Most work is integration, not creation
2. **Test-first development** - Catches issues immediately
3. **Clear gameplans** - No scope confusion

### Optional Work Protocol
Discovered that "if time permits" language causes agents to skip work. Solution: Explicit decision points requiring PM approval. This ensures thoroughness over speed.

### Testing as Discovery
PM testing reveals not just bugs but enhancement opportunities. Testing becomes a product development activity, not just validation.
