### On Architectural Complexity

The three patterns raise a critical question: Is this elegant domain optimization or accidental complexity?

**Evidence for "Good Complexity"**:
- Each pattern matches its domain's needs perfectly
- Slack (real-time events) → Granular for reactive coordination
- Notion (knowledge mgmt) → Embedded for analytical processing
- Calendar (time-based) → Delegated for protocol separation
- All three work without issues

**Evidence for "Bad Complexity"**:
- Three patterns = three maintenance paths
- Harder to onboard new developers
- Risk of pattern proliferation

**Verdict**: Acceptable IF documented well. The patterns emerged organically from domain needs, not arbitrary decisions. Document in ADR-038 with clear selection criteria.
