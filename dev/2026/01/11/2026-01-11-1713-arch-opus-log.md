## Phase 2: CIO Memo Analysis (5:45 PM)

### Summary of CIO's "Unihemispheric Dreaming" Proposal

The CIO raises a valid scaling concern: Piper's learning/dreaming system assumes predictable idle time. This assumption breaks for:
- Power users (frequent interactions, no gaps)
- Multi-user/team scenarios (always someone active)
- Dense scheduled jobs (background processing fills "idle" windows)

The dolphin metaphor suggests partial, rotating dreaming cycles rather than all-or-nothing idle-time dreaming.
